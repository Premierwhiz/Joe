Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our industrial storage solutions.

1. Do you manufacture custom rack dimensions?
Absolutely. We specialize in bespoke storage solutions. We map your facility and manufacture racks customized exactly to your height, width, and load-bearing requirements.
2. What is the load capacity of Heavy Duty Racks?
Our Heavy Duty Pallet Racks support anywhere from 1,000 KG to 4,000 KG per level (UDL), depending on the gauge of steel and the specific beam profile used.
3. Do you provide installation services pan-India?
Yes, we have a highly trained team of installation engineers who travel pan-India to ensure your racking systems are assembled safely and securely.
4. What materials are used for manufacturing?
We strictly use high-tensile, cold-rolled MS steel treated with an anti-corrosive powder coating finish for extreme durability and rust resistance.
5. How long does manufacturing and delivery take?
Standard orders are typically manufactured and dispatched within 10-15 business days, while custom mega-structures may take up to 4 weeks.
6. Is powder coating standard on all racks?
Yes, every rack undergoes a multi-stage chemical cleaning process followed by automated epoxy powder coating to ensure longevity.
7. Can I reconfigure the slotted angle racks later?
Yes! Our slotted angle racks use a modular nut-and-bolt assembly, meaning you can easily adjust shelf heights as your inventory changes.
8. What is the warranty on your storage systems?
We provide a comprehensive 5-year structural warranty against manufacturing defects under normal operational loads.
9. Do you assist with warehouse layout planning?
Yes, our CAD engineers can map your exact floor plan and provide 2D/3D visual blueprints before any manufacturing begins.
10. What is a Mobile Compactor and how does it save space?
It is a high-density racking system that moves on floor rails. By eliminating permanent aisles, it can increase your storage capacity by up to 50%.
11. Can cantilever racks be used outdoors?
Yes, if requested, we can apply a specialized hot-dip galvanized finish to our cantilever racks, making them completely weatherproof for outdoor lumber or pipe storage.
12. Are your mezzanine floors easily dismantlable?
Yes. Unlike welded structures, our Mezzanine floors are completely modular and bolted, meaning you can dismantle and relocate them if your facility moves.
13. How do you handle shipping to remote industrial locations?
We partner with robust heavy-freight logistics networks to ensure our steel components reach even the most remote industrial zones in India securely.
14. Do you provide load testing and safety certificates?
Absolutely. We are ISO 9001:2015 certified and can provide load-bearing safety certificates verified by structural engineers for your compliance audits.
15. What routine maintenance is required?
Minimal maintenance is needed. We recommend an annual visual inspection of base plates, anchor bolts, and beam safety pins to ensure forklift impacts haven't compromised the structure.
